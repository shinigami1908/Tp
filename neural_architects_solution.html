2. Problem Statement

Engineering managers face significant challenges when evaluating employee performance:

  • Data Fragmentation: Managers spend hours manually gathering inconsistent signals from Jira, GitHub, HR portals, and feedback forms.
  • Unconscious Bias: Without a unified data view, ratings are often influenced by recency bias or subjective interpretation of "hard work."
  • Lack of Actionability: Managers struggle to identify early warning signals (burnout, coasting) or provide data-backed justification for their ratings.

The Gap: There is currently no unified intelligence layer that transforms raw operational signals into fair, explainable, and future-focused performance assessments.

3. The Complete Solution

This platform is not just a dashboard; it is a Gen-AI powered Performance Intelligence Platform that acts as an objective "Fairness Auditor" and "Co-Pilot" for engineering leadership.

The solution consists of three core engines working in unison:

  1. The Unified ETL Engine: Automatically connects to Jira (Delivery), GitHub (Quality), LMS (Growth), and RTO logs to compute 75+ productivity KPIs without manual intervention.
  2. The Bias Detection Engine: Mathematically compares the "Expected Rating" (derived from data) against the "Manager Rating" (subjective input) to flag discrepancies like favoritism or harshness.
  3. The Generative Narrative Engine: Leverages GPT (via Tachyon) to synthesize complex datasets into professional, empathetic, and actionable performance reviews, while predicting future career trajectories.

5. Technical Architecture

The system follows a "Secure by Design" philosophy. No raw PII or CSV data leaves the secure backend. Only anonymized, structured contexts are transmitted to the LLM via the Tachyon Gateway.

End-to-End Workflow

  1. Data Ingestion (Simulation Layer): Ingestion adapters read from the Data Lake (simulating live Jira/GitHub/LMS streams for the hackathon).
  2. ETL & Periodization: Data is cleaned, aligned to fiscal periods (H1/H2), and normalized.
  3. Feature Engineering: Raw signals are converted into weighted KPIs.
  4. Scoring Engine: An "Expected Score" (0-100) is calculated based on organizational weights.
  5. AI Inference (Tachyon): The system constructs a secure prompt. The LLM performs a fairness check, rewrites narratives, and generates insights.
  6. Delivery: Final insights are serialized into JSON for the Manager Dashboard.

We utilize Generative AI (GPT) strictly as an interpreter and auditor.

B. Trend & Anomaly Detection

The engine compares H1 vs H2 metrics to provide context:

  • Positive Trend: KPI increase > 10% (e.g., "Velocity improved by 15% in H2").
  • Negative Trend: KPI decrease > 10% (e.g., "Quality dropped significantly in H2").
  • Anomaly Flag: Sudden drops in sentiment or massive spikes in defects trigger anomaly_flg = true.
